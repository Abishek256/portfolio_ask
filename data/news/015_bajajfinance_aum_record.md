# Bajaj Finance Q3 FY25: AUM Crosses ₹4 Lakh Crore, Asset Quality Stable

**Date:** January 29, 2025
**Ticker:** BAJAJFINANCE | **Sector:** Non-Banking Financial Company (NBFC)

Bajaj Finance reported a record Assets Under Management (AUM) of ₹4.04 lakh crore for Q3 FY25,
a 28% year-on-year growth. Net profit rose 18% to ₹4,308 crore, broadly in line with analyst
estimates. The company added 4.26 million new customers in the quarter, taking total customer
franchise to 92.7 million.

Gross NPA ratio was reported at 1.12%, marginally higher than 1.06% in Q2 FY25, reflecting
some stress in the consumer B2C lending segment where the company has been cautious about
disbursements following RBI guidance on unsecured lending. Management indicated that the
credit cost outlook for FY26 remains stable.

Net Interest Margin (NIM) for the quarter was 10.1% — significantly higher than banks due to
the higher risk profile of consumer loans — though it compressed 30 basis points versus the
year-ago period as funding costs rose and product mix shifted toward lower-yielding secured loans.
